Be the Voice of the Voiceless


We are the speech of the speechless. We are the voice of the voiceless. We are a group of animal lovers and advocates who have dedicated our lives to the animals in need. Knock down the barrier between humans and other living beings! Save the vulnerable.

    Save a Life, Save the World



By saving a life, be it human or any sentient being, we contribute to the preservation of the intricate tapestry of existence. Each life represents a unique thread, and the preservation of one is a testament to our shared responsibility for the collective well-being of our planet. If you are witness to any act of cruelty against an animal then you can complain to the local state SPCA (Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals) /Animal Welfare Organisations working in your area. Alternatively you can first contact a veterinary hospital to provide relief to the suffering animal.Police can be approached as they are the major enforcement body for the PCA Act 1960. Any offence against the animal those listed in Section 11 or 12 of the PCA Act can be reported to the police. You can also file an official complaint here -

      Feed them, don't hit them




Encouraging responsible and compassionate practices towards animals involves promoting feeding and caring for them rather than resorting to harm. Recognizing the intrinsic value of all living beings, advocating for the provision of food and nourishment aligns with ethical and humane treatment. Feeding animals rather than inflicting harm not only demonstrates empathy but also reflects our commitment to creating a world where kindness prevails over cruelty. This approach is not only ethically sound but also contributes to the broader goal of fostering a more compassionate and sustainable cohabitation between humans and the animal kingdom. Help us by donating funds for feeding hundreds of animals daily.
